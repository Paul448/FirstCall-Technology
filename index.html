About FirstCall Technology

FirstCall Technology is a trusted technology solutions provider based in Langata, Nairobi. We specialize in delivering high-quality tech products, including laptops, biometric attendance systems, CCTV cameras, and accessories. Our dedicated team offers expert installation and customized IT solutions tailored to meet the needs of businesses and individuals.

With a commitment to reliability and customer satisfaction, FirstCall Technology ensures that you get the best technology solutions to secure and grow your operations. Through continuous innovation and dedicated support, we aim to be the leading tech partner for Nairobi's businesses and communities.
